Our Mission

Empowering Nigerian Farmers with Solar-Powered Irrigation Growing Food Year-Round

AgriSolar empowers smallholder farmers to grow food year-round by providing affordable, portable solar-powered irrigation systems—eliminating the burden of diesel and the uncertainty of rain

We work at the intersection of climate resilience, financial inclusion, and agricultural productivity—bringing sustainable irrigation to the 90% of Nigerian farmland that currently lacks it.

Portable Solar Irrigation
Zero Upfront Investment

Our revolutionary revenue-sharing model eliminates financial barriers. Farmers pay nothing upfront and share revenue from increased yields, making solar irrigation accessible to all.

Community-Based Systems
Shareable & Portable

Designed for community use, our portable systems can be shared among multiple farmers, reducing individual costs while improving security and maximizing utilization.

Year-Round Food Production
Climate Resilience

Why Choose AgriSolar for Your Farm?

Our innovative approach addresses the unique challenges of Nigerian smallholder farmers,
combining proven technology with a revolutionary financing model.

  • Revenue-sharing—no upfront cost

    Our innovative financing model eliminates financial barriers. No initial investment required—farmers only pay from increased yields and profits.

  • Portable and secure for scattered plots

    Our mobile solar irrigation systems can be easily transported and secured between scattered farm plots, maximizing utilization and protecting your investment.

  • Designed for community sharing

    Built for collective use, our systems can be shared among multiple farmers in a community, reducing individual costs and improving accessibility.

  • Training & maintenance support

    Comprehensive training programs and ongoing technical support ensure farmers get maximum benefit and long-term reliability from their solar irrigation systems.
