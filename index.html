Our Mission

Empowering Smallholder Farmers in Nigeria
Solar-Powered Irrigation with Zero Upfront Cost

Only 10% of farmland in Nigeria is irrigated, and most farmers rely on expensive diesel pumps—if they can afford irrigation at all.
Through our innovative revenue-sharing model, AgriSolar provides solar-powered irrigation systems with no upfront cost,
making sustainable farming truly accessible and helping farmers escape climate-driven poverty.

Why Choose AgriSolar for Your Farm?

Our innovative approach addresses the unique challenges of Nigerian smallholder farmers,
combining proven technology with a revolutionary financing model.

  • Zero Upfront Cost

    Our revenue-sharing model eliminates financial barriers. No initial investment required—farmers only pay from increased yields and profits.

  • Portable & Shareable

    Designed for community use, our systems can be moved and shared among farmers, reducing individual costs and improving security.

  • Proven Technology

    Reliable solar-powered irrigation systems specifically adapted for Nigerian farming conditions and crop requirements.

  • Local Support

    On-ground technical support and training ensure farmers get maximum benefit from their solar irrigation systems.

  • Climate Resilience

    Break free from rain-dependent farming and diesel pump costs. Grow food year-round regardless of climate conditions.

  • Community Impact

    Empowering farming communities to escape poverty while building food security across Nigeria.
